Mississippi Pot Roast

Ingredients

  • Rump Roast
  • Dry Ranch Packet
  • Au Jus Gravy Packet
  • Pepperoncini peppers
  • 1/4 cup butter

Steps

  1. Place roast in slow cooker
  2. Add ranch and au jus packet to top of roast
  3. Add pepperoncini peppers
  4. Top with butter
  5. Cook on low for 8 hours
  6. Enjoy
