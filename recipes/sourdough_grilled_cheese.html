Sourdough Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread
  • 2 slices of velveeta cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter one side of bread, put butter side down on pan
  2. Add cheese slices to top
  3. Butter another piece of bread and put the butter side up
  4. Let side about 3-4 minutes on each side
  5. Enjoy the best grilled cheese that you have ever tasted
